Typical Pricing (Guidance)

Final quotes depend on usage, term, territory, and media. If you’re under a tight timeline, send the basics—I'll respond with options.

  • Indie / Student — starting at $50
  • Web / Podcast / YouTube — starting at $100
  • Short Film / Festival — starting at $150
  • Broadcast / Streaming / Campaign — custom quote
What you receive (deliverables)
  • WAV + MP3 (as needed)
  • Alt mixes (underscore / no drums / no bass / no lead, when requested)
  • Cutdowns (15 / 30 / 60 seconds, when requested)
  • Stems (grouped stems on request)
  • Metadata (clear titles, moods, cue-sheet-ready info)
Rights (non-exclusive vs exclusive)

Non-exclusive means the track remains available for other licenses. If a project requires exclusivity, I can quote an exclusive buyout / term-based license depending on scope.
