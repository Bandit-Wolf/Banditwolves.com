About the Orchestra

The Composer Behind
the Bandit Wolves

A cinematic, hybrid sound that blends orchestral soul, electronic body, and mind-bending storytelling.

the Orchestra

The Orchestra of Bandit Wolves is a narrative music project devoted to inner landscapes, mythic memory, and emotional depth in stories.

In a culture of speed and surface-level noise, it offers immersive soundscapes designed for reflection, presence, and inward exploration.

The music functions as a narrative language, shaping emotional cinematic music for contemplative experiences and storytelling.

the Composer

Kelvin Chico is a media composer and producer whose work draws from electronic production, orchestral study, DJ culture, and narrative exploration.

His compositions blend acoustic and synthetic elements to build emotionally resonant sonic worlds, offering music for reflection across film, television, games, and digital media.

Creative Philosophy

the Four Pillars

At its core, the Orchestra of Bandit Wolves treats music as more than accompaniment — it can help restore a sense of connection, clarity, and wonder.
The work is guided by four emotional pillars:

  • Poetic Resonance — grief, longing, and beauty

  • Mythic Wanderlust — exploration, awe, and ancient futures

  • Sacred Sensuality — ritual, embodiment, and desire

  • Transformative Growth — awakening, rebellion, and renewal


Each release explores these themes through sound, not to explain them, but to let them be felt.

From intimate underscores to expansive worldbuilding, the music emphasizes clarity of feeling, narrative depth, and musical detail

— designed to resonate rather than overwhelm.
